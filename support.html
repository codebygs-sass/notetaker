🔧 Troubleshooting

  • ✅ App not joining Zoom meetings? Make sure you've granted all requested Zoom permissions.
  • ✅ Not seeing notes? Refresh the dashboard or re-authorize your Zoom connection.
  • ✅ Audio transcription delayed? Check your internet connection and allow some processing time.

💡 FAQs

Q: Does Albetora NoteTaker record audio or video?

A: No. It only transcribes speech and stores meeting text notes securely.

Q: Can I export the meeting notes?

A: Yes! You can export as PDF, TXT, or Markdown directly from your dashboard.

Q: How do I revoke Zoom access?

A: Go to Zoom App Management and uninstall the Albetora NoteTaker app. We will remove your data within 30 days.
