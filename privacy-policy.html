Privacy Policy for Albetora Zoom App

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize and use the Albetora Zoom App, we may collect the following information:

  • Zoom Account Info: Name, Email, Zoom User ID, OAuth tokens
  • Meeting Data: Meeting ID, topic, time, participants, audio, and notes
  • Usage Data: Features used, session duration, logs for support

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate meeting notes and summaries
  • Allow editing, exporting, and viewing of notes
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide personalized support

3. Data Storage and Security

We use AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS for data in transit. Data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized systems only.

4. Data Retention

Your meeting notes are stored only as long as needed to provide services. You can delete them manually or email us to request account deletion.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We only share with trusted service providers under strict agreements, or if required by law.

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Revoke app access anytime
  • Email us to request complete data deletion

7. Revoking Access

You can uninstall the app from your Zoom account via your Zoom App Management Dashboard. Associated data will be removed within 30 days.
